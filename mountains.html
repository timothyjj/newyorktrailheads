Find a Mountain to Climb

We've covered several upstate New York mountain hikes that will take you to the top of Adirondack peaks. Most of these are within a reasonable driving distance of the Capital Region. While varying in difficulty and distance, all of these hikes provide good to excellent views from the top. You'll find trail descriptions, photos, and maps to help you decide on your next mountain climbing adventure!

  • Hadley Mountain
  • Shelving Rock Mountain
  • Sleeping Beauty Mountain
  • Spruce Mountain
  • Buck Mountain
  • Black Mountain
  • Prospect Mountain
  • Cat & Thomas Mountains
  • Poke-O-Moonshine
